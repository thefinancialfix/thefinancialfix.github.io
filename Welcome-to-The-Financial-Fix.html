Welcome to The Financial Fix

A little bit about me, the blog, and what I intend to write about.

Posted by Dan Fooks on September 23, 2019

Hello, and welcome to The Financial Fix! This blog’s sole purpose is to educate and inform the reader on various strategies for investing and saving money. I will discuss topics including investing, trading, budgeting, credit cards and credit, saving, and spending. Money management is extremely important to me, and it is a skill many do not have. Successfully managing your money is not difficult, but does require discipline. My first post, the one you’re currently reading, will cover some questions you might have as you explore my page.

Who does this blog cater to?

I have designed this website to cater to all levels of investors and savers. Whether you have shown an interest in money management all your life, or you are a first-time investor (or maybe you know nothing about investing or managing money), you will find benefit in reading this blog. I intend to write to cater to all audiences, spreading financial knowledge that anyone can learn from it. I am a very safe investor, which I will stress in future articles as I tell the reader more about myself and my trading habits.

How can I suggest a topic?

Located under the ‘Contact’ menu tab, you are able to send a message about anything you wish, including topic and blog post suggestions, or any general question about the topics I cover on this blog. I am here to answer questions, so never be afraid to ask! I will always send responses to message via email.

The site is still being constructed to add some great features, but in the meantime, I will be posting so our readers can enjoy content as we make improvements. More posts will be coming soon; welcome to The Financial Fix!
