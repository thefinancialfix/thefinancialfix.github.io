About Me

Investing and wealth management - This is what I do.


To the reader:

Hello, and thank you for visiting my page! My name is Dan, and I am a 20-year-old college student. I am currently working toward a BS in Computer Science, as well as a Minor in Business Administration. I am currently interning for a software security company as a Software Development and Quality Assurance Intern. I also work part time at a golf course, usually once a week. Aside from my two current jobs, I give piano lessons on the side, and also play guitar at restaurants as part of a duo over the Summer. Some ways I like to spend my free time include golfing, fishing, car maintenance, seeing friends, and spending time down the shore.

At the age of 16 years old, with the help of my dad, I opened up my first brokerage account, where I could begin to invest my hard-earned money. This is when I began to understand the value of the dollar, and what it can do for you over time when you put it to work. I began to develop savings goals, and realized the importance of saving at a young age. Since then, I have continued to pursue an interest in investing and personal finance, learning more and more about investing strategies and habits.

Although I am by no means an expert, I have found tremendous success in growing my money. I believe that investing is essential, and that anyone can learn to safely invest their money. The purpose of this blog is to share my success stories, what I have learned, and my strategies for growing and managing money.

Money is a tool, and it is essential we know how to use it for our greatest possible benefit.
