  Privacy Policy

The Financial Fix uses cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit thefinancialfix.co. This could include pop-up windows, or display preference features. The Financial Fix also uses third party advertisements on thefinancialfix.co to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when advertising on The Financial Fix, which will also send these advertisers (such as Google through the Google Adsense platform) information including your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Adobe Flash Player installed. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes (showing New York real estate ads to a user in New York, for example) or showing certain ads based on specific sites the user typically visits (such as showing cooking ads to a user who frequently visits cooking sites).

You can choose to disable or selectively turn off cookies on The Financial Fix or third-party cookies in your browser's settings. However, this can affect the user's ability to interact with certain elements of The Financial Fix, as well as other websites.
